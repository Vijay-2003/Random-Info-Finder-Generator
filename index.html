    • What is Random Generator ?

    A random generator is a device or a program that can produce one or many random numbers or items within a defined range or set. There are two main types of random generators: pseudo-random and true random. Pseudo-random generators use mathematical formulas to create random numbers, while true random generators use physical phenomena such as atmospheric noise or quantum events to create random numbers

    What is Random Facts Generator ?

    A random fact generator is an online tool that generates a random fact or facts list randomly. These facts can be about any topic, such as history, science, geography, or pop culture.
